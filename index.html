Welcome to our travel blog!

This blog will detail our trips throughout the years, as we go on a journey around the world.

SRI LANKA

For 2018, we decided to travel to Sri Lanka, a trip that was months in the making, and one that required meticulous planning. We started by taking advice from our friends who had gone to Sri Lanka before, and spoke to those who lived there already.

FLIGHTS

We used Google Flights, starting from June to late July, when we eventually booked the ticket. We set up a lot of trip trackers on Google Flights throughout this period, and we found Colombo to be consistently one of the cheapest destinations. After lenghty discussions regarding the Phillippines and other countries in South East Asia, we finally decided on Sri Lanka our destination. Saudia had flights for £336 per person, which was a great deal.
